REDCLIFF CHRONICLES

Welcome! Your are been handpicked selected and invited to the first User Test of Redcliff Chronicles.

Goal

You control a 4-men strong squad, which tries to surviving on the deadly MARS. An all destroying Sandstorm is hunting your squad, try to survive by reaching the next basecamps.

Controls

When your turn starts, you see a list of abilities in the bottom
Press the according Number on your keyboard to select an ability
Press the same Number to cancel your action.
Press Left/Right Mouse button to select tiles or enemies.

Gameplay

Every Unit has two actions per turn, with different Action costs, which influence the unit order.
  1. Move the unit inside their movement range.
  2. Attack a enemy inside the combat range.
  3. IntAttack: Use your Intensity for your powerful attack.
  4. Rest: Convert your Intensity to Will.
  5. Loot: Loot items from the ground.
  6. Rage: Sacrifice Will to gain Intensity.
